About

Battery researcher with 6+ years of specialized expertise. Pioneered Prussian blue cathode stabilization (>76% retention over 1000 cycles), nitrogen-doped carbon anodes (3× graphite capacity), and PVDF-HFP composite separators (372% electrolyte uptake, high ionic conductivity, thermal stability). Collaborated with Nobel laureate Prof. John B. Goodenough. Published 21 SCI-indexed papers and filed 2 patents on battery innovations.
